Catering for Your Next Event!

Let Pine Grill & BBQ bring the authentic taste of Southern BBQ to your next gathering! Whether it's a corporate lunch, a family reunion, a wedding, or a casual get-together, our team is ready to provide delicious, hassle-free catering that will leave your guests raving.

We offer a variety of customizable options to suit any size event and budget. From our signature slow-smoked brisket and pulled pork to our mouth-watering sides and desserts, we ensure every dish is prepared with the same passion and quality you expect from our grill.

Our Catering Packages Can Include:

  • Main Meats: Brisket, Pulled Pork, Ribs, Smoked Chicken, Sausage
  • Sides: Mac & Cheese, Coleslaw, Pit Beans, Cornbread, Collard Greens, Potato Salad
  • Sauces: Our homemade BBQ sauces (Sweet & Tangy, Spicy, Mustard-based)
  • Desserts: Peach Cobbler, Banana Pudding
  • Beverages: Sweet Tea, Lemonade, Bottled Water

We can accommodate various service styles, including drop-off, buffet setup, or full-service options. Let us handle the food so you can focus on enjoying your event!
